#1 - SOS barnbyar

  • Client & Brief

    How to get 25-30 years old to foundraise?

    SOS Children's Villages is an independent, non-governmental international development organisation that protects the interests and rights of children. The brief was how to get 25-30 years old to foundraise money to the organization, with a digital solution.

  • We did

    The option to donate in an easy way

    an app that helps a person to save money that they usually spend on unnecessary things, yet it also gives them the option to donate in an easy way and contributes with the knowledge what money acctually can be worth for someone in need.

  • Process

    People have the ambition and economy to donate, but few actually does it.

    A one week project where we collected interwievs and find that most people have the ambition and economy to donate, but few actually does it. Donating money to a cause is usually a satisfying experience but it’s seldom fun. As a consequence people tend to postpone their donation, which in worst case could lead to no donation at all.

  • Result

    1 minute movie, presenting how the app works
